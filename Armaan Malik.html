Armaan Malik

Armaan Malik (born 22 July 1995) is an Indian singer, songwriter, record producer, voice-over, performer, dancer and actor.
He is known for his singing in multiple languages, including Hindi, Telugu, English, Bengali, Kannada, Marathi, Tamil, Gujarati, Punjabi, Urdu and Malayalam.

Malik made his debut as a playback singer in the Hindi film Jai Ho, singing "Tumko Toh Aana Hi Tha."
In 2016, he received the Filmfare R. D. Burman Award for New Music Talent.
Malik provided the voice of Aladdin in the Hindi-dubbed version of Disney's Aladdin, a live-action remake of the 1992 film.
He sang "Pehla Pyaar" for the film Kabir Singh, under the composer Vishal Mishra.

Malik signed with Arista Records in March 2020, and on March 20 of that year, he released his first English-language single, "Control".
He became the first artist to reach No. 1 on the Top Triller Global Billboard charts twice in 2020.
In 2021, he appeared on A. R. Rahman's single "Meri Pukaar Suno," which was released in June 2021.
For his single "You," he won Best India Act at the 2022 MTV Europe Music Awards.
