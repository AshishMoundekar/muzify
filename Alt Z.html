Recommended Artists

Alec Benjamin

Alec Shane Benjamin is an American singer-songwriter from Phoenix, Arizona.
Benjamin's album Narrated for You reached number 127 on the US Billboard 200 in 2019. It features the songs "Water Fountain", "Outrunning Karma", and "Let Me Down Slowly"

Anson Seabra

An emotive singer/songwriter, Anson Seabra makes artfully crafted and philosophical, piano-driven pop.
He emerged online in 2018 and garnered a strong social media fan base with songs like "Broken" and "Emerald Eyes."

Sabrina Carpenter

Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter is an American singer and actress.
In 2014, her debut music single "Can't Blame a Girl for Trying" and EP of the same name were released.

Rosa Linn

Rosa Linn, is an Armenian singer-songwriter.
She started her career by getting signed by Nvak Collective and releasing her debut single "King" with Kiiara in 2021.
